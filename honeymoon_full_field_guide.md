# May 11 — Zurich Arrival → Bern

## Day Strategy

This is a recovery day. The goal is:

- Get from Zurich Airport to Bern smoothly.
- Check into or drop bags at Hotel Goldener Schlüssel.
- Eat something easy.
- See Bern Old Town without forcing a full tourist day.
- Be asleep at a reasonable hour.

You will be jet-lagged. Treat the day as a soft landing.

---

## Leg 1 — Arrive Zurich + Train to Bern

### Timing
- **Flight arrives Zurich:** 10:50 AM
- **Suggested airport buffer:** 10:50–11:45 AM
- **Suggested train window:** around 12:15 PM
- **Arrival Bern:** around 1:15 PM

### Locations
- **Zurich Airport Railway Station**
  - Address: Zurich Airport / Zürich Flughafen, 8058 Zürich-Flughafen, Switzerland
  - The train station is inside the airport.
- **Bern Railway Station**
  - Address: Bahnhofplatz 10A, 3011 Bern, Switzerland

### Step-by-step
1. Exit plane and follow signs for arrivals / passport control.
2. Since you are carry-on only, skip baggage claim unless something changes.
3. Follow signs for **Train / Bahn / SBB**.
4. Zurich Airport station is directly connected to the terminal.
5. Buy tickets at an SBB machine or use the SBB Mobile app.
6. Look for a direct InterCity train to Bern.
7. No seat reservation needed.
8. Trains run frequently, so do not panic if you miss one.

### Helpful notes
- The station is not a separate shuttle ride; it is part of the airport complex.
- If tired, prioritize simplicity over perfect train timing.
- Get water or a snack before boarding if needed.

### If X then Y
- **If passport control is slow:** take the next train. Do not rush.
- **If you feel disoriented:** follow SBB / Bahn signage, not taxi signage.
- **If you are hungry before the train:** grab something quick at Zurich Airport and still eat a better lunch in Bern.

- **Address:** Rue des Musées 29, 2300 La Chaux-de-Fonds, Switzerland

### Route / Areas

- **Address:** Avenue Léopold-Robert, 2300 La Chaux-de-Fonds, Switzerland

- Good for shops, cafés, and city grid feeling.

#### Villa Fallet
- **Address:** Rue de la Serre 15, 2300 La Chaux-de-Fonds, Switzerland
- Early Le Corbusier work.
- Good architecture stop.

#### Espacité Tower
- **Address:** Espacité 1, 2300 La Chaux-de-Fonds, Switzerland
- Optional viewpoint / orientation point.

### Don’t Miss
The city grid itself. The wide streets and layout were tied to watchmaking and light-filled workshops. This is the “hidden” point of the city.

### Shopping notes
- Expect small shops and local boutiques, not Geneva-style luxury shopping.
- If you find vintage watch shops, browse casually and do not feel pressured.
